SoraVé

SoraVé is a complete web platform built with Ruby on Rails to connect vehicle owners with people looking to rent a luxury vehicle in a secure, fast, and easy way. The main goal is to offer users the experience of traveling in style.

¿Looking for luxury vehicles?

The reservation interface is intuitive. Users can clearly see the availability of vehicles in an interactive calendar, select desired dates, and proceed with payment—all in just a few steps

Filter your choices...

To enhance the search experience, we implemented a powerful filtering system that allows users to find the perfect vehicle based on location, type, model, and price range.

Look for the details...

Each vehicle has its own detail page where users can view multiple photos, read a full description from the owner, check reviews from other renters, and view all technical specifications.

Book a date...

The final booking process is transparent and secure, showing a full cost breakdown before confirmation..

LOGIN OR CREATE AN ACCOUNT...

Users can easily sign up or log in to manage their vehicles, bookings, and personal profile.
